Favorite Foods and Games

Today I'll be talking about some of my Favorite Foods and Games.

  1. Foods:

    • Daal Chawal
    Classic Rice and Lentils dish. Its so simple yet so appetizing. It's weird that prisoners are fed this here in Pakistan, I think it's too good to be served in prison. But ig that's the beauty of it. It's so simple, easy and cheap to make that you can basically eat anywhere. Alot of people also add in a whole lot of veggies, I personally just like plain boiled rice and lentils (which are neither too thick or too thin).
    • Seekh Kebabs
    The best type of Kebabs to ever exist. I cant even describe how good they are.
    • Halwa Puri
    I absolutely love this breakfast. You've got your channay (Chickpeas), your puri (that bread ball looking thing), your chai (tea duh) and halwa (a sweet dish). It goes so hard on winter mornings, although I would say it can be harmful for health so another one you can try which is kinda similar is Naan/Roti Channay and a Fried Egg along side it.
    • Gannay ka Juice
    This is Sugarcane juice, and its the best drink for hot summer days, you'll 100% get a brainfreeze by drinking it but its worth it.
    • Doodh Jam e shirin
    This is Milk + Rose Water Syrup and I usually drink this at nights before sleeping. It always satisfies you no matter If you drink it hot in the winter or ice cold in the summers.
